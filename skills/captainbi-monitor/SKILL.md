# CaptainBI Monitor

Read `captainbi-shared` first.

- Business report: `cbi monitor business-report`
- Review summary: `cbi monitor bad-review-summary`
- Reviews: `cbi monitor reviews`
- Feedback: `cbi monitor feedback`
- Followup monitor: `cbi monitor followup`
- Hijacked records: `cbi monitor hijacked-record`
