# CaptainBI Ads

Read `captainbi-shared` first.

- Campaigns: `cbi ads advertise-campaign`
- Ad groups: `cbi ads advertise-group`
- Keywords: `cbi ads advertise-keyword`
- Campaign report: `cbi ads campaign-report`
- Search term reports: `cbi ads search-term-placement-report`, `cbi ads search-term-keywords-report`
