# CaptainBI Sales

Read `captainbi-shared` first.

- Orders: `cbi +orders --open-channel-id <id>`
- Returns: `cbi sales returns`
- Refunds: `cbi sales refunds`
- FBM shipping upload is a dangerous write command; preview with `--dry-run`.
