# CaptainBI Goods

Read `captainbi-shared` first.

- Shops: `cbi +shops` or `cbi goods shops`
- Sites: `cbi +sites` or `cbi goods sites`
- Goods list: `cbi +goods --open-channel-id <id>` or `cbi goods list`
- Goods extensions: `cbi goods items`
- Tags and groups: `cbi goods tags`, `cbi goods groups`
- Write operations: `set-group`, `edit-group`, `set-operate-user`, `set-shop-operation-mode`
